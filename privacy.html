Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026.

This Privacy Policy explains how Postiz ("we", "us") processes information when you use our self-hosted social scheduling application and connect third-party social accounts via OAuth.

Information we process

  • Account information you provide: email, company/name (if entered), and basic profile details needed to use the app.
  • OAuth tokens: access/refresh tokens for connected providers, used only to perform actions you request (connect, schedule, publish, fetch basic account data).
  • Content you upload: media files (videos/images) and post text you choose to schedule/publish.
  • Usage and logs: basic logs and diagnostics used to operate and secure the service.

How we use information

  • Provide core functionality: account linking, scheduling, publishing, and status tracking.
  • Operate and secure the service: prevent abuse, troubleshoot failures, and maintain reliability.
  • Comply with legal obligations when required.

Sharing

We share data only as needed to deliver the service:

  • Social platforms: content and required metadata are transmitted to connected providers when you publish.
  • Service providers: infrastructure services (e.g., hosting/storage/email) if enabled by the self-host operator.

Retention

We retain uploaded media and scheduled posts for as long as the self-host operator keeps them, or until you delete them. OAuth tokens are retained while an integration is connected and may be removed when you disconnect the provider.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data, including restricting access and using encryption in transit where applicable. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your choices

  • Disconnect social integrations to revoke future access.
  • Delete posts and uploaded media where supported by the application.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the site operator for this deployment.
